# Team Plan Sport PERPAMSI

## Tim Ideal

- Product Owner: keputusan scope, prioritas, aturan event.
- Lead Architect/Tech Lead: arsitektur, data model, security, technical decision.
- Project Manager/Scrum Master: timeline, blocker, koordinasi, rilis.
- UI/UX Designer: flow public, admin, panitia, prototype.
- Frontend Developer 1: public page, live score, jadwal, bracket, ranking.
- Frontend Developer 2: admin panel dan panel panitia.
- Backend Developer 1: auth, role, master data, peserta, dokumen.
- Backend Developer 2: match, skor, bracket, klasemen, ranking, audit log.
- QA Engineer: test plan, regression, UAT, load test sederhana.
- DevOps/Infrastructure: deployment, domain, SSL, backup, monitoring.
- Data/Event Coordinator: data PDAM, cabor, venue, peserta, jadwal, aturan ranking.
- Support Event: standby saat event dan bantu panitia.

## Tim Minimum

- 1 Product Owner atau Project Manager.
- 1 Tech Lead.
- 2 Fullstack Developer.
- 1 UI/UX Designer.
- 1 QA paruh waktu.
- 1 DevOps paruh waktu.
- 1 Data/Event Coordinator.

## Ritme Kerja

- Daily check-in 15 menit.
- Scope review dua kali seminggu.
- Demo internal setiap akhir minggu.
- UAT panitia mulai fase stabilization.
- Freeze fitur sebelum go-live.

## Ownership

- Product Owner memutuskan prioritas dan trade-off.
- Tech Lead menyetujui data model dan arsitektur.
- UI/UX menjaga standar tampilan dan flow.
- QA menjaga checklist release.
- DevOps menjaga production readiness.
- Data/Event Coordinator menjaga kelengkapan data event.

## Support Event

- 1 admin teknis standby.
- 1 support panitia standby.
- 1 PIC data/skor final standby.
- 1 jalur eskalasi issue production.
